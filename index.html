About Sustainify

This project, Sustainify, is developed as a part of my semester minor project. The aim of this website is to help people discover sustainable alternatives to everyday products that are commonly harmful to the environment. Our world is facing serious issues like plastic pollution, excessive waste, and overuse of non-renewable resources. Through this website, users can easily search and explore eco-friendly products such as reusable bottles, bamboo toothbrushes, cloth bags, and many more. The website is designed with a user-friendly interface, smooth transitions, and a clean layout to provide a good browsing experience. JavaScript dynamically handles product cards, searching, and bookmarking features. This project demonstrates how web development and technology can be used for promoting sustainability and spreading environmental awareness.
